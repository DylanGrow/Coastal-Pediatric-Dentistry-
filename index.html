Welcome to Coastal Pediatric Dentistry!

Superior oral health is vital for a child's development, and we're here to help every step of the way.

Coastal Pediatric Dentistry is a leading pediatric dental practice in New Bern, North Carolina. We love children, and we have the knowledge and experience needed to provide your family with child-friendly, early dental care.

You can rest assured that we will work hard to make sure your family's dental experiences with us are completely positive and stress-free!
